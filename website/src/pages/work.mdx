# Work With Me

## Helping you through the messy middle
There are times in a project where things slow down. Maybe you have an ambitious idea, but don't know what the next concrete steps should be. Maybe your team is scaling, but you don't have time to create the onboarding and team building you want to do. Maybe you've been carrying so much in your head for so long, you have no idea how to get it all out. 

Maybe you've tried using Claude to speed things up, but it’s created more noise than signal. AI is so much more powerful when partnered with human expertise that can validate, course-correct, and avoid rabbit holes.

I work with leaders to turn ambiguity into purposeful action. I excel at sitting down with subject matter experts and change makers to help them articulate their vision in a way that is grounded and scalable. We'll create documents, processes and systems that honor your vision and invite others into the collaboration. 

When I write technical documentation or bug bash a product, I analyze it intensely from every direction, from functionality, risk, legislative requirements to user experience. I facilitate processes to help organizations innovate, renew, and rally around a shared goal.
